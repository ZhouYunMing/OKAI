Usage Tips

Hi! Welcome to Project OKAI! Here are some usage tips for the optimal experience:

  • For browsers, Chrome and Safari work the best!

  • Once you get to a chapter, keep scrolling down to view the content, and click on the left or right arrow icon to switch between chapters and pages.

  • Whenever you see bold texts , hover over them to view additional information and explanation.

  • For quick navigation, click on the hamburger icon on the top left corner to open the side menu.
